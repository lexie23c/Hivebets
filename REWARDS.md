# HiveBets Rewards System

Earn rewards on every bet and build passive income through referrals.

## Overview

HiveBets offers two reward systems:

1. **Cashback** - Earn on every bet you place

Both systems are automatic, instant, and stack with your betting winnings.

## Cashback System

### How It Works

Every time you place a bet on HiveBets, you earn cashback:

1. **Place a Bet** - Use gasless x402 betting or traditional betting
2. **Instant Calculation** - Cashback is calculated immediately
3. **Automatic Credit** - Added to your claimable balance
4. **Claim Anytime** - Withdraw your cashback whenever you want

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When do I receive cashback?
Instantly when you place a bet. It's automatically added to your claimable balance.

### Do I earn cashback on losing bets?
Yes. You earn cashback on every bet, win or lose.

### How long do referral earnings last?
Forever. You earn from every bet your referrals make, for life.

### Can I refer myself?
No. Referral links are tied to wallet addresses and self-referrals are not allowed.

### Is there a maximum cashback amount?
No. Earn unlimited cashback based on your betting volume.

### How many people can I refer?
Unlimited. There's no cap on referrals or referral earnings.

### Do referrals know I referred them?
No. Referral relationships are private and on-chain.

### Can I change my referral link?
No. Each wallet has one unique referral link.

### What if my referral uses gasless betting?
You still earn commission. x402 gasless betting doesn't affect referral earnings.

### Are rewards paid in BNB?
Yes. All rewards (cashback and referrals) are paid in BNB.
